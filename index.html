About

I am a musician, creative engineer, and programmer based around Baltimore, MD. Originally a classical guitarist and teacher by trade, my current practice explores the limits of and bridges the gaps between sounds and visuals.

Interested in collaborating? Reach out to me!

Sights and Sounds
  • by/go (2022) - various sounds and compositions between 2015 and 2021
  • Live AV improv for SNIPS (2021) - datamoshed home movies, tape loops, metaphors about finding new meaning in memory through obsolete technology
  • Audiovisual improvs (2021) - various synths, video synths, and glitches
  • Tinitic (2017) - guitar + audio reactive DAW built in Max/MSP
