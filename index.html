Alex Taylor

About

I am a tinkerer, artist, and musician based in MD with a background in classical guitar and teaching. My practice includes collaged electro-acoustic improvisations, mixed media visuals, and unintended computer-driven collaboration.

Interested in collaborating? Reach out to me!

Sounds
  • dendroism (2025) - soundscapes and compositions exploring legacy and loss
  • by/go (2022) - various sounds and compositions between 2015 and 2021
  • remixes for exploring (2020) - glitchy ambient remix of a great track by MEMZ
  • Tinitic (2017) - guitar + audio reactive DAW built in Max/MSP

Sights
  • Live AV improv for SNIPS (2021) - datamoshed home movies, tape loops, metaphors about finding new meaning in memory through obsolete technology
  • Audiovisual improvs (2021) - various synths, video synths, and glitches

Other Stuff
  • Wordpress Blog to Twitter Bot - made for The Last Space Blog
  • Inclined Inclinations - hackable creative prompt generator using context free grammar
  • Glitch Toolz - a growing collection of scripts for mangling sounds and video
  • Optical Hijinks - an art class I teach on creatively misusing technology
